Local Candles By Electra Jane

Eclectic Eye is a small business local to Harrisonburg, VA that specializes in beautiful homemade candles. We craft each candle carefully with locally harvested plants and ethically sourced crystals. We are partnered with Horizon Gifts, another local shop, and our candles are available for purchase in their store. Custom candles are also available via our custom order form.
